About this website



Insight Q&A uses topic modeling and word embedding techniques to allow you to answer your data science questions searching among conversation shared online by Insight Alumni.

After entering your question, Insight Q&A searches through a 4 year long database of online conversations looking for those Q&A threads that most closely match your query. Once identified, Insight Q&A shows you these threads, ordering them by how well they match your input. If you choose to 'Browse by topic', Insight Q&A will show you a list of relevant data science topics and the most relevant Q&A threads associated to them.
